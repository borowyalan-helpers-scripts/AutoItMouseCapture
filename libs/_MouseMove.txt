###User Defined Function###
_MouseMove

###Description###
Moves the mouse smoothly, with some random movements.

###Syntax###
#include <SmootherMouseMove.au3>
_MouseMove ($iX, $iY)

###Parameters###
@@ParamTable@@
$iX
	Position to move to in X offset.
$iY
	Position to move to in Y offset.
@@End@@

###ReturnValue###
None.
